Female Subject (希りんご)　(Rin) BiB//D:_R - 2.1 - Kitchen

She enters the kitchen,
the smell of rot and nature unpleasently mix.

She looks around at the old withered appliances and notices the
pantry and shelves still have some books and trinkets.

She rummages through the old shelves, she's looking for a particular book. She stops and idles for a moment as she reminisces on the home cooked meals of her childhood.

There should be a note for her, though likely not in the kitchen; she remembers something, her father used to tell her stories from a particular book over dinner. It was his favorite book, he always kept it in his office upstairs.

At a side glance as she leaves the room, she notices a peculiar shelf, there are some perfectly healthy apples. Strange.

somehow it is fresh,
but it still smells like house rot.
Grab an apple? Or Go upstairs?

Leave?
