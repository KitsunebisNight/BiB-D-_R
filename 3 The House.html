Female Subject (希りんご)　(Rin) BiB//D:_R - 2 - Home

All but the enterance of the house
seems to have been swallowed by nature.

She creaks open the front door and enters the house. Anxious at the
recollection of her life, she distracts herself with reminiscent memories.
She remembers the layout all too well,
Living room to the left. Kitchen to the right.

She decided to search
The living room? Or The kitchen?

Go home?
